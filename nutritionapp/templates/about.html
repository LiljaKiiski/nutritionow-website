About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals to make informed decisions about their health by providing easy access to answers for their health-related questions. We believe that healthy eating is a fundamental component of ones welbeing, and our app is designed to support everyone in making positive choices in their nutrition. Our ultimate goal is to provide everyone with helpful information, to help them reach their health goals.

Why NutritioNow?

Our program provides quick answers to many questions that you may have. Due to the fact that our model uses AI, our model can generate answers much faster than a human could. However, our model i not to be trusted above a medical professional. For serious concerns, always contact your doctor for help.
